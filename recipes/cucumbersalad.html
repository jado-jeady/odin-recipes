Dad's Creamy Cucumber Salad Recipe

Description:

Dad's Creamy Cucumber Salad is a refreshing and tangy side dish that perfectly complements any meal. This recipe combines crisp cucumbers, onions, and a creamy dressing to create a delightful summer salad. The cucumbers provide a cool and crunchy texture, while the creamy dressing adds a creamy and tangy flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh dill or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced cucumbers and onions.
  2. In a separate smaller bowl, whisk together the sour cream, mayonnaise, white vinegar, sugar, salt, and black pepper until well combined.
  3. Pour the creamy dressing over the cucumbers and onions. Gently toss to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Before serving, give the salad a quick stir. Garnish with fresh dill or parsley, if desired.
  6. Serve Dad's Creamy Cucumber Salad chilled as a refreshing side dish alongside grilled meats, sandwiches, or as a light standalone salad.
